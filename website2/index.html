Our Story

Where Every Meal
Feels Like Home

Butter & Cup Café is a cozy destination where friends and families gather to enjoy homemade meals, desserts, and fresh coffee in a welcoming environment.

Every recipe has been crafted with love and care, using locally sourced ingredients wherever possible. We believe great food starts with great ingredients — and even better company.
